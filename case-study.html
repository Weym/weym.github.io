Design System

A minimalist design language focused on high contrast and functional typography to highlight the developer's work.

Typography

Montserrat

Geometric Sans-Serif • Headers & Titles
Used for visual impact and structure.

Poppins

Humanist Sans-Serif • Body Text
High readability for long-form reading.

Space Mono

Monospace • Data & Details
The code's technical signature.

Color Palette

Code Logic // color-mix(in srgb, var(--stone), var(--black) 20%);

The palette is dynamic. Intermediate shades are generated live by the browser, mixing the Secondary color into the Primary color at varying percentages.

A "raw" aesthetic built with code-first precision. Turning simple hex codes into a tactile digital experience.
