ABOUT ME

(Who I am)

Driven by curiosity, fueled by bolo de rolo. Based in Recife, Brazil.

I’ve spent my time training dogs, crafting websites, and even building a house. Random paths that share a single thread: an obsession with taking an idea and making it a reality. When I’m not making things, I’m usually exploring other worlds as a reader who sees the little things in history, philosophy and fiction, a gamer, and a student of new languages or struggling through chords on a guitar I haven't quite conquered yet.
